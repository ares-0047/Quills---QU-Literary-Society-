What is Literature?

Literature is the mirror of humanity. It is the art of written work that spans poetry, prose, drama, and non-fiction. Beyond just words on a page, it is the preservation of culture, history, and the deepest emotions of the human experience. It allows us to step into lives we have never lived and eras we have never seen.

Importance of Literature in Youth

For young minds, literature is a catalyst for empathy and critical thinking. It challenges preconceived notions and encourages readers to question the world around them. Engaging with literature at a young age builds articulate communicators, creative problem-solvers, and individuals who possess a profound understanding of diverse perspectives.

Role of Literary Societies in Universities

A university literary society like Quills is a sanctuary for expression. We provide a platform beyond academic rigor where students can freely explore their artistic inclinations. By fostering debate, encouraging public speaking, and hosting creative spaces, we build a community that celebrates intellectual curiosity and the power of narrative.
